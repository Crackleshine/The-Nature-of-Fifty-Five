Interview Log 343-█: The ██████ Enquiry

Interviewee: SCP-343

Interviewer: Dr Kant

Foreword: Interview with SCP-343 to ascertain the nature of SCP-055. Based on an analysis of timestamps in the recordings of Researcher [REDACTED] interview with SCP-343. There has been noted to be a discrepancy in the time taken to ‘describe’ what SCP-055 is. This interview aims to clarify SCP-343’s description of SCP-055.


[BEGIN LOG]

Dr Kant: I would like to make it clear that I’ll prefer to stick to conversing within the constraints of language.

SCP-343: Of course my son. Has it ever struck you as odd that your division has both a [REDACTED] and Kant? And yet you insist on using science as a means to arrive at knowledge. I'll have you know I don't deal in coincidences.

Dr Kant: We are exploring all possibilities at the moment SCP-343.

*SCP-343 darkens*

SCP-343: Men have died for making light of my signs, Kant.

Dr Kant: We mean no disrespect SCP-343.

SCP-343: Call me Father, but of all the progress you have made I would think that that nature of SCP-055 is best discussed with Descartes, the Greeks and even your predecessor, Kant.

Dr Kant: SCP-343, we have observed that in [REDACTED] interview, each time [REDACTED] enquired about the nature of SCP-055 the gap in which its nature is communicated verbally differs could you explain why?

SCP-343: That is…intriguing. Clearly the █████ you know as SCP-055 is a ██████████.


*Kant is informed through his communicator that the time taken to describe SCP-055 differs from previously observed attempts.*


Dr Kant: Father can you repeat what you just said?


*SCP-343 is observed to be distracted*


SCP-343: I mean it is a ███

Dr Kant: Father…

SCP-343: It is a ████████████████████


*SCP-343 is observed to be, uncharacteristically, visibly distressed, he tries to name SCP-055 multiple time, its description changes on each naming*


Dr Kant: Father! Please have mercy!

SCP-343: YOU WANTED THIS BOY, YOU WANTED TO KNOW IT IS– ███████████████████████████████████████████████
